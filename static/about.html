About the Author

Hello! I'm Surabhi M — a passionate learner and technology enthusiast exploring the world of DevOps and software engineering.

This project reflects my journey of applying DevOps concepts like CI/CD, containerization, orchestration, and GitOps using tools such as Docker, Kubernetes (EKS/Minikube), Helm, Argo CD, and GitHub Actions.

Beyond my projects, I work as a Technical Trainer specializing in Java and Python. I love simplifying complex topics for learners and guiding them toward hands-on understanding.

I also contribute to open-source initiatives — still a newcomer, but learning every day. My goal is to keep growing and give back to the developer community that helped me learn.
